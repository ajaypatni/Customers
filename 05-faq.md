# ❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Answers to the most common questions from users.

---

## 🔐 Account & Login

**Q: I forgot my password. What do I do?**  
Click **Forgot Password** on the login page, enter your email, and follow the reset link sent to your inbox. The link expires after 30 minutes.

---

**Q: I'm not receiving the verification/reset email.**  
Check your **spam or junk folder** first. If it's not there, make sure you're using the correct email address. You can request a new email after 5 minutes.

---

**Q: Can I change my email address?**  
Yes. Go to **Settings → Profile → Email Address**, enter your new email, and verify it via the confirmation link sent to the new address.

---

**Q: Can I have multiple accounts?**  
One account per email address is allowed. If you need a separate workspace, ask your admin to create a team account.

---

**Q: How do I log out?**  
Click your **profile avatar** in the top-right corner and select **Log Out**.

---

## 📁 Files & Data

**Q: Is there a file size limit for uploads?**  
Yes. The maximum file size per upload is **25 MB**. For larger files, consider compressing them or splitting into parts.

---

**Q: What file types are supported?**  
Commonly supported formats include:
- Documents: `.pdf`, `.docx`, `.txt`
- Images: `.jpg`, `.png`, `.gif`, `.webp`
- Spreadsheets: `.xlsx`, `.csv`
- Presentations: `.pptx`

---

**Q: Can I recover a deleted item?**  
Deleted items are moved to the **Trash** for 30 days before permanent deletion. Go to **Trash** from the sidebar to restore them within that window.

---

**Q: How do I download my data?**  
Go to **Settings → Privacy → Export My Data**. A download link will be emailed to you within 24 hours.

---

## 👥 Sharing & Collaboration

**Q: Why can't my colleague see the item I shared?**  
Make sure you shared with their correct email address and that they accepted the invite. Also check that you granted the right permission level (View, Comment, or Edit).

---

**Q: Can I share with someone who doesn't have an account?**  
You can generate a **public link** for view-only access without requiring login. Go to **Share → Get Link → Anyone with the link**.

---

**Q: How many people can I share with?**  
There is no hard limit on the number of people you can share with. However, some plans restrict the number of **editors** per item.

---

## ⚙️ Settings & Preferences

**Q: How do I change the language?**  
Go to **Settings → Profile → Language** and select from the available options. The page will reload in the selected language.

---

**Q: Can I use the system in dark mode?**  
Yes. Go to **Settings → Appearance → Theme** and select **Dark Mode**, or toggle it from your profile menu.

---

**Q: How do I turn off email notifications?**  
Go to **Settings → Notifications** and set email delivery to **Off** for each notification type, or switch to **In-App Only**.

---

## 💳 Billing & Plans

**Q: How do I upgrade my plan?**  
Go to **Settings → Billing → Upgrade Plan** and choose the plan that suits your needs.

---

**Q: Can I cancel my subscription?**  
Yes. Go to **Settings → Billing → Cancel Subscription**. Your access continues until the end of the current billing period.

---

**Q: Will I lose my data if I downgrade?**  
No. Your data is preserved. However, features exclusive to higher plans will no longer be accessible.

---

## 🆘 Support

**Q: How do I contact support?**  
Click the **Help** icon (?) in the bottom-left corner and select **Contact Support**. You can also email support directly.

---

**Q: Where can I report a bug?**  
Go to **Help → Report a Bug**, describe the issue, and attach a screenshot if possible. Our team typically responds within 1–2 business days.
