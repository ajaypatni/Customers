## System Requirements

Before you begin, make sure you have the following:

- A modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge)
- A stable internet connection
- A valid email address for registration

---

## Creating Your Account

1. Go to the sign-up page
2. Enter your **full name** and **email address**
3. Choose a strong password (at least 8 characters)
4. Click **Create Account**
5. Check your email for a verification link and click it

> ⚠️ **Note:** The verification email may take up to 5 minutes. Check your spam folder if you don't see it.

---

## Logging In

1. Visit the login page
2. Enter your registered **email** and **password**
3. Click **Sign In**
4. If you've forgotten your password, click **Forgot Password** and follow the instructions sent to your email

---

## Navigating the Interface

Once logged in, you'll see the main dashboard with these key areas:

| Area | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Sidebar** | Quick access to all main sections |
| **Top Bar** | Notifications, profile settings, and search |
| **Main Area** | Your primary workspace |
| **Help Icon** | Access support and documentation anytime |
