# 📝 Basic Usage Guide

Learn how to perform everyday tasks and get the most out of the system.

---

## Overview

This guide covers the most common actions users perform day-to-day. Each section includes step-by-step instructions you can follow immediately.

---

## 1. Creating Something New

To create a new item:

1. Click the **+ New** button in the top-right corner
2. Choose the type you want to create from the dropdown
3. Fill in the required fields (marked with `*`)
4. Click **Save** to confirm

> 💡 **Tip:** You can also press `Ctrl + N` (Windows) or `Cmd + N` (Mac) as a keyboard shortcut.

---

## 2. Editing Existing Items

1. Find the item you want to edit (use Search if needed)
2. Click on it to open it
3. Click the **Edit** button or double-click the field you want to change
4. Make your changes
5. Click **Save Changes** — or **Discard** to undo

> ⚠️ Changes are not auto-saved unless indicated. Always click **Save**.

---

## 3. Searching & Filtering

Use the **Search Bar** at the top of the screen:

- Type a keyword and press `Enter`
- Use **Filters** to narrow results by date, category, or status
- Click **Clear Filters** to reset

### Search Tips

| Want to find | Search like this |
|--------------|-----------------|
| Exact phrase | `"quarterly report"` |
| Items by a person | `author: Priya` |
| Items from a date | `date: 2025-04-01` |
| Items with a tag | `tag: urgent` |

---

## 4. Deleting Items

1. Select the item(s) you want to delete
2. Click **Delete** or press the `Delete` key
3. Confirm the action in the popup dialog

> ⚠️ **Deleted items may not be recoverable.** Always double-check before confirming.

---

## 5. Exporting Data

1. Select the item or list you want to export
2. Click **Export** from the menu
3. Choose your format: `PDF`, `CSV`, or `Excel`
4. The file will download to your device

---

## 6. Sharing with Others

1. Open the item you want to share
2. Click the **Share** button
3. Enter the recipient's email address
4. Choose their permission level:
   - **View** — can only read
   - **Comment** — can add notes
   - **Edit** — can make changes
5. Click **Send Invite**

---

## 7. Using Notifications

Notifications appear in the **bell icon** on the top bar.

- Click the bell to see all recent alerts
- Click a notification to go directly to the related item
- Click **Mark All as Read** to clear them

---
