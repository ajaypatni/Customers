# ⚙️ Settings & Customization Guide

Personalize the system to match your preferences and workflow.

---

## Accessing Settings

Click your **profile avatar** in the top-right corner and select **Settings** from the dropdown menu.

---

## 1. Profile Settings

Update your personal information:

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Display Name** | The name others see |
| **Profile Photo** | Upload a photo (JPG/PNG, max 2MB) |
| **Email Address** | Used for login and notifications |
| **Time Zone** | Ensures dates and times display correctly |
| **Language** | Change the interface language |

**To update your profile:**
1. Go to **Settings → Profile**
2. Edit the relevant fields
3. Click **Save Profile**

---

## 2. Password & Security

### Changing Your Password
1. Go to **Settings → Security**
2. Enter your **current password**
3. Enter and confirm your **new password**
4. Click **Update Password**

### Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)
Strongly recommended for account security:
1. Go to **Settings → Security → Two-Factor Auth**
2. Click **Enable 2FA**
3. Scan the QR code with an authenticator app
4. Enter the 6-digit code to confirm
5. Save your backup codes somewhere safe

---

## 3. Notification Preferences

Control what you're notified about and how:

1. Go to **Settings → Notifications**
2. Toggle on/off each notification type:

| Notification | Options |
|--------------|---------|
| New comments | Email / In-app / Off |
| Mentions | Email / In-app / Off |
| Updates | Email / In-app / Off |
| Reminders | Email / In-app / Off |

3. Set your **Digest Frequency**: Instant, Daily, or Weekly

---

## 4. Appearance

### Theme
- **Light Mode** — Default bright interface
- **Dark Mode** — Easier on the eyes in low light
- **System Default** — Follows your device setting

**To change:** Go to **Settings → Appearance → Theme**

### Text Size
Adjust font size for readability:
- Small / Medium (default) / Large

---

## 5. Privacy Settings

| Setting | What It Controls |
|---------|-----------------|
| **Profile Visibility** | Who can see your profile |
| **Activity Status** | Show/hide when you're online |
| **Data Sharing** | Opt in/out of usage analytics |

Go to **Settings → Privacy** to manage these.

---

## 6. Connected Accounts

Link external accounts for easier access:

1. Go to **Settings → Integrations**
2. Click **Connect** next to the service you want
3. Follow the authorization steps
4. Once connected, the status changes to ✅ **Connected**

To disconnect: Click **Disconnect** next to the service.

---

## 7. Resetting to Defaults

If something feels off and you want a fresh start:

1. Go to **Settings → Advanced**
2. Click **Reset to Default Settings**
3. Confirm in the dialog

> ⚠️ This resets appearance, notifications, and preferences — but does **not** delete your data.
